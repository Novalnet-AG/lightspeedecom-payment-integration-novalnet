= 3.1.0 - 2025.12.05 =

* Fix - Novalnet configuration is now controlled based on the respective shop ID
* Fix - Payment status handled for refund and instalment cancellation transactions
* Enhanced - Date of birth validation regulated on checkout page
* Enhanced - Novalnet payments will be displayed individually on the checkout page based on the payments activated in the Novalnet Admin Portal
* Enhanced - API/Webhook Response is normalized in the payment app for Instalment Payments
